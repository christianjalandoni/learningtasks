Top 10 Fave Movies

Christian Jalandoni

  • The Truman Show

    A man discovers that his entire life has been a reality TV show and decides to break free from the constraints of the show's world.

  • The Hunger Games

    In a dystopian society, teenagers are forced to compete in a televised fight to the death as a form of entertainment for the wealthy ruling class.

  • Babylon

    Follows the rise of Hollywood's golden age and the transition from silent films to talkies.

  • La La Land

    A musical romance about a struggling actress and a jazz musician trying to make their dreams come true in Los Angeles.

  • Parasite

    A darkly comedic thriller about a poor family who infiltrates the lives of a wealthy family in Seoul, South Korea with unexpected and shocking results.

  • White Chicks

    Two FBI agents go undercover as white women to solve a kidnapping case in this comedy film.

  • Birdman

    A former Hollywood superhero actor tries to revive his career by directing and starring in a Broadway play while struggling with his own personal demons.

  • Avatar

    A paraplegic Marine is sent to a distant planet to infiltrate the native Na'vi people, but his experiences there lead him to question his loyalty to humanity.

  • Star Wars: A New Hope

    The original classic sci-fi adventure film that follows a group of rebels as they try to destroy a powerful weapon known as the Death Star and restore freedom to the galaxy.

  • Interstellar

    A team of astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet as Earth faces a catastrophic food shortage and environmental collapse.
